1. Digitization

The fundamental process of encoding analog information into the zeroes and ones that computers can process. It's about format.

Strategic Takeaway

"Your first step isn't just about scanning; it's about inventorying core information. Identify data that, if made digital, unlocks the most insight."

Purpose

To encode information in computers by converting it from analog to digital format.

Outcome

Deals mainly with recording data that can eventually be used by digital technologies.

2. Digitalization

Using digitized data to improve existing business processes and workflows. It's about process efficiency.

Ex
Online Banking

Real-time money transfers replacing manual, in-person workflows.

Ex
Automated Insights

Automatically generating customer behavior reports from digitized source data.

Why Digitalize?

  • Increase operational efficiency
  • Reduce operational costs
  • Minimize human errors

3. Digital Transformation

The profound integration of digital technology into all areas of a business, fundamentally changing how it delivers value.

Case Study: Spotify

Reimagined the music industry based on access over ownership, creating a completely new business model.

Case Study: Modern Workplace

Replaces disconnected, paper-based workflows with integrated ecosystems of mobile and web apps.

Strategic Impact

Customer

Elevated experience through extreme personalization.

Market

Accelerated innovation and reduced time-to-market.
